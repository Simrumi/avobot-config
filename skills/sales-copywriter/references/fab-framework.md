# FAB Framework - Features-Advantages-Benefits

Transforms technical specs into compelling value propositions.

## Structure

### 1. Features (F)
The factual, objective attributes of your product/service.
- **Technical specs**: What it IS
- **Physical characteristics**: Size, capacity, components
- **Functionality**: What it DOES
- **Inclusions**: What's in the package

**Feature Examples:**
- "24/7 automated monitoring"
- "500GB cloud storage"
- "12-module video course"
- "30-minute coaching calls"

### 2. Advantages (A)
What the feature does better/differently than alternatives.
- **Comparison**: How it beats competitors
- **Performance**: Speed, efficiency, quality
- **Convenience**: Time saved, effort reduced
- **Reliability**: Consistency, dependability

**Advantage Translation:**
- Feature: "24/7 monitoring" → Advantage: "Never miss an issue, even while you sleep"
- Feature: "500GB storage" → Advantage: "Store 5 years of data without upgrading"
- Feature: "12 modules" → Advantage: "Complete system from beginner to advanced"

### 3. Benefits (B)
The emotional and practical outcomes the customer receives.
- **Save time**: More freedom, less busywork
- **Save money**: Better ROI, reduced costs
- **Reduce stress**: Peace of mind, confidence
- **Gain status**: Recognition, prestige
- **Achieve goals**: Dreams realized, problems solved

**The "So What?" Test:**
Always ask "So what?" until you reach emotion:
- Feature: "Automated backups"
- So what? → "You never lose work"
- So what? → "You sleep peacefully knowing everything is safe"

**Benefit Hierarchy:**
1. Functional benefits (what they can do now)
2. Emotional benefits (how they feel)
3. Identity benefits (who they become)

## Application Formula

**Full FAB Statement:**
> "Our [feature] means you [advantage], which gives you [benefit]."

**Example:**
> "Our 24/7 automated monitoring means issues get caught instantly, which gives you the peace of mind to finally take a real vacation without checking your phone."

## Best Used For:
- Technical products needing translation
- Feature-heavy software/services
- Comparison pages
- FAQ sections
- Product descriptions

## Key Principles
- **Lead with benefits, support with features**
- **Features answer 'what', benefits answer 'so what'**
- **Stack benefits: functional → emotional → identity**
- **Every feature must map to a clear benefit**
