# Headline Formulas & Templates

The headline is 80% of the work. These formulas generate high-converting headlines.

## Universal Formulas

### 1. The How-To Headline
**Template:** How to [Achieve Desired Outcome] Without [Objection/Pain]

**Examples:**
- "How to Write Sales Copy That Converts Without Spending Years Learning Copywriting"
- "How to Lose 20 Pounds Without Giving Up Your Favorite Foods"
- "How to Double Your Revenue Without Working More Hours"

### 2. The List Headline
**Template:** [Number] [Adjective] Ways to [Desired Outcome]

**Examples:**
- "7 Proven Headlines That Generated $10M+ in Sales"
- "3 Simple Tweak That Tripled Our Conversion Rate"
- "21 Copywriting Mistakes That Are Costing You Sales"

### 3. The Secret Headline
**Template:** The [Adjective] Secret to [Desired Outcome]

**Examples:**
- "The Surprising Secret to Writing Copy That Sells"
- "The 'Hidden' Strategy Top Copywriters Use (But Never Talk About)"
- "The Uncomfortable Truth About Why Your Sales Page Isn't Converting"

### 4. The Question Headline
**Template:** [Intriguing Question That Implies a Problem]? + [Promise of Solution]

**Examples:**
- "Tired of Sales Pages That Don't Convert? Here's the Fix."
- "What If Writing High-Converting Copy Was Easier Than You Think?"
- "Are You Making These 5 Fatal Copywriting Mistakes?"

### 5. The Contrarian Headline
**Template:** Stop [Common Advice]. Start [Better Approach].

**Examples:**
- "Stop Writing 'Benefit-Driven' Copy. Start Using This Instead."
- "Forget Everything You've Learned About Headlines. Do This."
- "The Best Sales Copy Doesn't Sound Like Sales Copy"

### 6. The Curiosity Gap Headline
**Template:** [Known Fact] + [Unknown Element That Creates Tension]

**Examples:**
- "I Spent $50,000 on Copywriting Courses. Here's What Actually Works."
- "The Headline That Generated $1M (And Why Nobody Uses It)"
- "I Fired My Copywriter. Then Sales Doubled."

### 7. The Time-Specific Headline
**Template:** [Outcome] in [Surprisingly Short Timeframe]

**Examples:**
- "Write a High-Converting Sales Page in 2 Hours (Not 2 Weeks)"
- "The 15-Minute Copywriting System That Outperforms Agencies"
- "Get 10 Years of Copywriting Wisdom in 48 Hours"

### 8. The 'Little Known' Headline
**Template:** The [Little-Known/Unknown/Unusual] [Method/Trick/Strategy] to [Outcome]

**Examples:**
- "The Little-Known Headline Formula That's Generated $100M+"
- "An Unusual Copywriting Trick That Works in Any Market"
- "The 'Boring' Headline Style That Actually Converts Better"

## Headline Power Words

**Urgency:**
- Now, Today, Immediately, Instantly, Fast, Quick, Rapid
- Limited, Scarce, Running Out, Last Chance, Final

**Exclusivity:**
- Secret, Hidden, Insider, Proven, Exclusive, Members-only
- Elite, Premium, Proprietary, Behind-the-scenes

**Results:**
- Guaranteed, Proven, Tested, Verified, Scientific
- Effortless, Easy, Simple, Automatic, Done-for-you

**Emotion:**
- Shocking, Surprising, Stunning, Jaw-dropping, Controversial
- Essential, Critical, Vital, Must-have, Non-negotiable

## Headline Checklist
- [ ] Does it promise a specific benefit?
- [ ] Does it create curiosity or urgency?
- [ ] Is it clear in 2-3 seconds?
- [ ] Would it make ME want to read more?
- [ ] Does it speak to a specific pain or desire?
- [ ] Can it be shorter and punchier?

## Testing Rules
1. Write 10-20 variations minimum
2. Test different angles (fear vs. greed vs. curiosity)
3. Get feedback from target audience
4. A/B test top 2-3 contenders
5. Never settle for your first draft
